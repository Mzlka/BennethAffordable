about us

Making Home Ownership Possible for Every Filipino Family

At Benneth Affordable House and Lot, we believe every Filipino family deserves a safe, comfortable, and affordable home. Based in Novaliches, Quezon City, we have helped hundreds of families find their perfect property across Metro Manila and the provinces.

From first-time homebuyers to OFW investors, we offer a wide range of properties with flexible payment terms and personalized support — from your first inquiry all the way to the day you receive your keys.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and accessible real estate brand in the Philippines — making home ownership a reality for every Filipino family, regardless of budget.

Our Mission
  • Connecting families with quality, affordable properties
  • Providing honest, transparent real estate guidance
  • Making Pag-IBIG & bank financing accessible
  • Supporting OFWs with 100% online processing
  • Building trust through every successful home sale
